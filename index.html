A NOTE ABOUT EASE OF PRESENTATION:

Only DVD &  Blu ray discs have the ability to have custom Chapter Markers and Indexes added to them. This means you can select which videos play and from what point in time. Disc Authored programming gives you, the client, the option to select exactly where your videos starts to play.  

MPEG-2 & MPEG-4 digital files - Do Not Have Chapters or Indexes. This means for example: if you want to watch a scene 15, 40, or 55 minutes into the video, you will have to fast forward the video until it gets to that point.  

When played on your computer, laptop, tablet, or Smart phone, the video can be scrubbed: meaning you can slide the playback indicator, of the video player, to any point in the video or just click on any point in the Timeline to jump to that playback point.
