Eric Flakes

Professional Wedding & Family Events Videographer

Video Elegance Productions

My name is Eric Flakes, I grew up right here in Dayton, Ohio. I started and operated this Wedding & Family Events Video Production business in San Diego, California from 1991 to 2000.

My late father passed away here in 2015. He was a prolific amateur photographer & videographer, but he left behind hundreds of videocassette tape recordings, Super 8 film home movies, and thousands of Photographic prints & slides, which he had made little or no attempt to label, date, catalog, or leave any documentation as to when, where, who or what all these recording were about.

My Objective: 

to help you leave too your family, and future generations, a concise narrative of your family's History, Traditions & Heritage captured in your home photographs, slides, movie films, & video recordings. In a digital file format, your photographic materials will have a new life.
